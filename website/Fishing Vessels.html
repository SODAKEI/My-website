Fishing vessel- These vessels are found the middle value of measured ships that are essentially utilized for getting fish and other marine untamed life. Beside business fishing, they can likewise be utilized for relaxation cruising. Large number of fishing vessels are being utilized to get fish for individual or business purposes everywhere.

There are two general classifications of fishing vessels. They are:
  • Factory Ship-hese ships function as fish processing vessels. They are big ships with all the necessary onboard facilities for the freezing and processing of all sizes of fish, including whales.
  • Purse Seiners/Trawlers-Fishing trawlers, also called draggers, are commercial fishing vessels that can operate as pure trawlers. A fishing method that involves active pulling or dragging a trawl through the water is called trawling. In this operation, one or more trawlers will do the dragging of the net.
