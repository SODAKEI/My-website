Naval Ship- Naval ships are vessels created specifically for military use. For the most part due to their intended role, naval ships are constructed differently from civilian ships. These ships typically have armament systems. In order to protect them from damage from outside forces, they are also fashioned of stronger materials.

There are maritime ships explicitly made for maritime fighting, and they are called warships. Ships made for help capabilities are called assistant boats. These boats are planned exclusively for shipyard tasks and seldomly utilized for troop and combat hardware transport.
