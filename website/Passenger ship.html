Passenger Ship- A vessel is already categorized as a passenger ship if it can accommodate more than 12 people. These boats must adhere to all inland water and maritime travel laws. The response of governments worldwide to reduce marine disasters involving passenger ships is strict adherence to certain maritime laws..

Passenger ships have two general classifications:
  • Ferry- It is used for transporting passengers on short distances
  • Cruise Ship-This seagoing vehicle is mostly used for recreational travel. Most of these ships visit specific tourist destinations all around the world. Cruise ships compete with one another by providing guests with top-notch entertainment, hotel-style accommodations, and a variety of foods and beverages
