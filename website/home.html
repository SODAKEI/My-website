SHIP- A ship is a big boat that navigates the oceans and other sufficiently deep rivers, either carrying cargo or people, or in support of more specific missions like fishing, research, or defense. According to size, shape, load capacity, and purpose, ships can be distinguished from boats.

The role of shipping in the progress of humanity as a whole is important. It connects producers and suppliers, sellers and buyers. Maritime transport is therefore considered one of the most important transport activities.
